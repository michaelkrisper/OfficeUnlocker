OfficeUnlocker

Remove editing protection from Office and Outlook files — in your browser.

FILE

Files never leave your device. Removes sheet/workbook/document protection (Office & OpenDocument), VBA and Outlook .pst passwords. It doesn't crack real encryption — it just removes restrictions; files locked with a real password are detected, not decrypted. Your original is never changed.
